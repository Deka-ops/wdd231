Site Plan: Abia Delights

A branding and content plan for a traditional Nigerian cuisine website.

Site Name

Abia Delights

This name represents a food brand that highlights traditional dishes and culinary experiences from Abia State, Nigeria. It was chosen to reflect cultural authenticity and local pride.

Optional domain availability: abia-delights.ng

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to share and celebrate traditional Igbo meals, provide menus and ordering info, highlight local ingredients, and allow users to explore the story behind each dish. A contact form and business inquiries page will also be included.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular dishes offered at Abia Delights?
  • How can I place an order or contact the chef directly?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #283618 – used for body text and borders
  • Secondary Color: #bc6c25 – used for headings and call-to-action buttons
  • Accent Color: #dda15e – used for borders and highlights
  • Background Color: #fefae0 – page background

Typography

  • Heading Font: Playfair Display elegant serif font for all headings
  • Body Font: Roboto clean sans-serif for readability in paragraphs and menus
